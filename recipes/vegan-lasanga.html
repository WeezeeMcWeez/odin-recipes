Veggie Lasanga

Surprisingly Tasty Vegan Lasanga

When I get into "health mode", meat (which is something I already don't eat much of) is the first thing thrown from my diet. During my first vegan experiment I created a vegggie lasagna, basically substituting mashed potatoes and black beans for meat (I'm allergic to soy, so tofu isn't an option). Ya know, it wasn't too bad!

Now that meal has become somewhat of a staple at our dinner table four to six times a year. Try it out, homes!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • Ragu pasta sauce
  • Shredded mozzerella AND shredded cheddar cheese
  • Your favorite brand of instant mashed potatoes
  • 1 can of black beans
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic Salt
  • Butter (salted)
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna nooldes accoriding to packaging instructions. When finished, mix them with a little bit of oil so that they don't stick together.
  2. Make four servings of instant mashed potatoes according to directions on box. Add seasonings to taste.
  3. Rinse black beans.
  4. Spread a bottom layer of noodles in a 13 x 9 pan.
  5. Spread a layer of potatoes, a layer of black beans, and a layer of mozzerella on top of the noodles.
  6. Repeat this for three layers of noodles. On the top layer, add both mozzerella AND cheddar cheeses
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for about 15 minutes, uncovered.
