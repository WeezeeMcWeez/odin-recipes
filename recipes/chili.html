Grandma Leora's Chili

Average Chili But I Like It So Keep Your Mouth Shut

When it's cold out and I want something that's pretty darn filling without a ton of Calories, but will also definitely give me heartburn, I like to make this chili. To be clear: it is not special. Well, it could be if you season it right, but that will be up to you. The ingredients are simple, and so is the recipe.

Let's get to it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 can Campbell's tomato soup
  • 1 can of Hunt's tomato sauce (usually about 14 ounces)
  • Chili powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic Salt
  • Cumin
  • Onion and green pepper
  • 1 can of red kidney beans

Steps

  1. Brown the meat.
  2. Rinse them kidney beans
  3. Combine tomato soup, two additional tomato-soup-cans of water, and tomato sauce to a boil.
  4. Combine meat and beans and turn heat down medium-high.
  5. Once chili has reached desired thickness, season to taste (usually quite a bit of chili powder)
  6. Add chopped onions and peppers and continue to heat for ten minutes.
  7. Add Young's Jersey Dairy cheese curds for extra delicious-ness.
