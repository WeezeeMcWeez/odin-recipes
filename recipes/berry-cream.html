Berry Cream

Making Fruit Tolerable

One of the most overrated food groups in the world are fruits, and specifically, "berries". Strawberries are trash. The texture of a blackberry is akin to that of a cockroach. But at least they aren't a hairy fruit like the raspberry! And don't get me started on blueberries, those sour excuses for treats.

That's where Ruth's Chris saved me one day. If you smash a bunch of egg yolks and sugar and cream together--you get something pretty tasty! So with that--here ya go!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 4 egg yolks

Steps

  1. Start by bringing a pot of water to a boil. Complete next steps while water heats up.
  2. Separate egg yolks from whites and put into a large bowl.
  3. Mix egg yolks with half the sugar; mix the other half of the sugar with heavy cream
  4. Heat cream/sugar mixture until boiling
  5. Slowly add cream/sugar mixture to egg yolk/sugar mixture while stirring
  6. Now that all ingredients are mixed-use the pot of (now-boiling) water as a double boiler to heat the mixture while you mix continuously. Should take 10-15 minutes or until the mixture has thickened to custard-like consistency.
